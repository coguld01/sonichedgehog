Instructions for sonic the hedgehog

When playing the game on a computer you us arrow keys.
When playing Clicking on sonic one time will make him spin
with a sonic game sound. Then you keep clicking him and he will keep spinning around and around.

  • Power sneakers make sonic run like the wind.
  • Super rings gives Sonic 10 rings at once.
  • One-ups give sonic 1 more extra life.
  • An invincible helps you protect you from enemies.
  • Shield protects sonic form damage but only 1 time only.

At the end of all the zones Dr.Robotnik attacks you with a robot machine. The goal is to defeat him and free some of your friends.
The game ends when you squash Br.Robotnik and he won’t come back. You need to hit Robonik around eight times to defeat him. Dr. Eggman’s
weakness is lacking in power, his stamina is very weak.
